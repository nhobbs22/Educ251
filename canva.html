Canva

What is Canva?

Canva is a free online graphic design platform. Canva is an easy way to create digital content. that allows you to to design anything such as comic strips, logos, business cards, interactive powerpoints, and more.

4th Grade Lesson Overview


Summarizing teaches children how to dissect important information from text. By asking a student to summarize, you can check to see if they understand how to pull apart main ideas from details that may contribute to the experience of the story, but not the overall meaning. Creating a comic strip is a fun way to check to see if a student understands the overall meaning of a story. I will teach students how to create a summary using a comic strip on canva where each image will represent a different part of the plot: exposition, conflict, rising action, climax, and resolution.

CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RL.4.2

Determine a theme of a story, drama, or poem from details in the text; summarize the text.

Universal Design for Learning

Provide Multiple Means of Representation & Expression Using a comic strip to teach summarizing helps students who need multiple means of representation, a visual option for a written summary. It will allow students to see the important aspects such as rising action and climax through imagery. Having students create these images will give them multiple means of expression of a summary. This will also help with their memory retention.
