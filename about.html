About Me

My name is Natasha Hobbs and I aspire to be and Elementary School Teacher(grades 1st-6th).


I enter my educational career excited and ready to offer children a customized education that benefits each of my students' needs. Prior to education, I have pursued careers in makeup design as well as UX Design. I believe my training in user experience has given me a unique perspective in becoming an educator. As a UX Designer, I am trained to think beyond the limits of my own needs and instead research and evaluate the needs of my user, or in this case now, my students. 


Psychology courses such as Human Factors and Cognition have trained me to understand how salience, attention, and visual search impact the way we process information. This understanding allows me to design lessons that help my students grasp the material being presented. This is especially essential when designing plans for diverse learners. 
By integrating technology, we can help create experiences that maintain students' attention and activate their working memory in order to retain information. While technology is a great tool, it is also important to understand the negative impacts that can occur if not integrated and monitored properly. Courses such as “The Internet and Our Psychological Health” have helped me recognize the importance of teaching technology in an responsible and ethical manner.


I am excited to embark on this journey combining UDL and blended learning in the classroom in order to provide students with the necessary resources and tools to navigate their learning.
