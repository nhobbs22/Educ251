Twisted Wave

What is Twisted Wave?

Twisted Wave is an online audio editor that allows you to make record audio and make edits directly in your web browser. If you choose to record your audio elsewhere, you can upload your audio files and convert it to mp3.

2nd Grade Lesson Overview


While reading aloud benefits a student by increasing their memory retention, recording students reading aloud can actually increase their efficiency and learning speed. Having students record their own voices while reading helps them become advocates of their own learning. They can pick up on errors they may not realize they are making whether it be with pronunciation or word recognition. It can also help students become better storytellers. I will have my students record themselves reading using twisted wave as they get to become narrators.

CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.SL.2.5

Create audio recordings of stories or poems; add drawings or other visual displays to stories or recounts of experiences when appropriate to clarify ideas, thoughts, and feelings.

Universal Design for Learning

Provide Multiple Means of Representation, Expression & Engagement: Having students record and listen to themselves provides multiple means of representation by representing written stories, auditorily. It provides multiple means of engagement by having students engage with written text by speaking and listening. It provides multiple means of engagement by providing options for self regulation. It does this by having students listen and check themselves for errors.
