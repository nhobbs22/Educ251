iMovie

What is iMovie?

iMovie is a video editing software created by Apple that allows you to bring your video recording to life by adding special effects, transitions, subtitles, and more. iMovie also allows you to cut and edit scenes so your story is told fluidly.

6th Grade Lesson Overview


In 6th grade Social studies, students will gather in small groups to plan and create a video displaying their understanding of key features in Ancient Egyptian Art and Architecture. Having students create video will help them learn how to identify important features, and creatively display those features visually and auditorily to tell the story. We are entering a world where digital content is taking over. It is important that educators provide children with the skills to navigate their learning through digital story telling.

CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RH.6-8.7

Integrate visual information (e.g., in charts, graphs, photographs, videos, or maps) with other information in print and digital texts.

Universal Design for Learning

Provide Multiple Means of Representation & Expression: Having students collaborate as a team to create a video provides multiple means of representation by displaying learned content in a visual, and audible manner. This lesson provides multiple means of engagement by fostering collaboration and community.
