Google Docs

What is Google Docs?

Google docs is an online tool that allows you to create documents as well as edit and collaborate on said document in real-time. Google docs also allows you to to draw shapes and create visual diagrams by selecting and formatting shapes and inserting text as needed.

5th Grade Lesson Overview


Comparing and Contrasting is an important tool to teach students. It helps students understand how two ideas can relate or differ from one another. It also helps students learn how to organize and recall information. Using graphic organizers such as venn diagrams are a great way to teach students how to compare and contrast. Using google docs, students will collaborate with small groups to create a venn diagram comparing and contrasting two characters from their reading.

CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RL.5.3

Compare and contrast two or more characters, settings, or events in a story or drama, drawing on specific details in the text (e.g., how characters interact).

Universal Design for Learning

Provide Multiple Means of Representation & Expression: Creating graphic organizers helps students create a visual organization of their ideas. Working with a small group provides options for expression and communication. By having studnents create a Venn Diagram collaboratively, students get a change to verbally expresss their ideas, auditorily process their peers, thoughts, and visually display the comparisons that they are making.
